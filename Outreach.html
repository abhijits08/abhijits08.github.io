Outreach

I am passionate about contributing to the communities I live in. I serve as a Resident Assistant at one of the NUS student accomodations, and engage in volunteering programs as a mentor for school children in my locality.

I am particularly keen on contributing to holistic education of children, normalizing seeking help for mental health issues, and finding effective solutions for issues faced by the elderly. Please do reach out if you have interesting ideas about improving our communities, as I would be glad to have a discussion with you.
